Petrified Forest National Park
-----------------------------
#### *Arizona, 2022*
![Petrified Forest National Park](assets/petrified.jpeg){: style="width: 100%"}

ASU students Amy Zhao and Libby Berkhout brought their scope along for a trip
to the Petrified Forest to try it in a very low RFI environment (compared to
the Phoenix area).
While their data didn't turn out great, they learned about the importance of
rehearsing the setup before taking the horn out into the wild.
And they got some really cool pictures!
You can read about their adventure in
[their memo](memos/2022.06.25_PetrifiedForest.pdf).
